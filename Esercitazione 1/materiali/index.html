Focaccia with Caramelized Onions,Cranberries, and Brie

Wow your test buds with this festive focaccia appetizer topped with sweet caramelized onions, tart cranberries, and creamy brie.It will became your new favorite holiday starter

Image: Sally Vegas

FEATURED IN

Fresh cranberries recipes that go beyond sauce

Ingredients

For the focaccia

  • 4 cup (500gr) all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 teaspoon instant yeast
  • 1 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 3/4 cups (365gr) lukewarm water
  • 1/4 cup extra olive oil, plus more for the pan

For the topping

  • 3 medium yellow onions, thinly sliced
  • 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
  • 1-2 tablespoons olive oil,for brushing on the top
  • 10 ounces brie, cut into cubes ( scrape rind off with a paring knife if desired, though it's not necessary)
  • 1 1/2 cups fresh or frozen cranberries

Method

  1. Make the focaccia dough

In a medium-size mixing bawl,or in a stand mixer with a paddle attachment, mis togheter all the dought ingredients untile the flour is fully adsorbed. You don't need to actually knead the dought, thought I sometimes knead it a few times in the bowl when mixing by hand to incorporate the of the flour...
